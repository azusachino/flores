## Core Concepts

1. Write from abundance
    1. read a lot
    2. get rid of those with bad contents
2. Write from conversation
    1. talk to your family, best friends
    2. talk to your audiences
    3. talk to your editor
3. Write in public
4. Imitate, then innovate
